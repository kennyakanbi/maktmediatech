Professional Team United by Vision & Dedication

At Mak Media Tech, we are more than just a media and technology brand, we are a team of passionate innovators, creators, and problem-solvers. Founded in Lagos with a vision to bridge creativity and technology, our journey has been driven by the belief that every brand deserves the right tools and strategies to thrive in the digital age.

From our early beginnings, we set out to redefine how businesses connect with their audiences. Today, we specialize in media advertising campaigns, software development, video & live stream production, and media/tech training, offering solutions that combine creativity, precision, and measurable impact.

What truly sets us apart is our team. Each member brings unique expertise, from strategic thinkers and creative designers to technical developers and production specialists, all united by one mission: to empower brands and individuals through innovative media and technology solutions.
